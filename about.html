About Cowboy Systems

Building the future of physical therapy software

Cowboy Systems is a healthcare operating system built exclusively for physical therapy and wellness practices — giving clinicians their time back so they can focus on what matters: patient outcomes.

Our Mission

We believe physical therapists should spend their time treating patients, not wrestling with outdated software. Cowboy Systems was built from the ground up to eliminate documentation burden, streamline billing, and give clinic owners real visibility into their business — all from a single, modern platform.

We're not a generic EHR retrofitted for PT. We're purpose-built for the way PT clinics actually work.

Why We Exist

Physical therapy has one of the highest documentation burdens in healthcare. Therapists routinely spend 2–3 hours per day on notes, billing paperwork, and administrative tasks that take them away from patients and drive burnout.

We built Cowboy Systems to change that. Our AI-assisted documentation, intelligent scheduling, and automated billing workflows are designed to give those hours back — to the clinician, to the patient, and to the practice.

What We Build

Cowboy Systems is an all-in-one platform covering:

  • AI Documentation — voice-to-SOAP notes generated in seconds
  • Scheduling — intelligent calendar with waitlist and automated reminders
  • Billing & Claims — PT-specific billing codes, automated claim submission
  • Patient Records — unified digital charts, e-intake, secure portal
  • Analytics — real-time revenue, outcomes, and provider productivity dashboards
  • Telehealth — built-in video visits with session notes pre-loaded
